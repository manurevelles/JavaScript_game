Have you tried the game already? Are you having problems reaching the end of the game? Hopefully this might be helpful!

WARNING: The following guide contains spoilers. You might want to stop reading if you want to discover things for yourself.

--- WALKTHROUGH ---

1. The gate to the castle of the Dark Lord is blocked due to two artifacts: ice and fire. To deactivate them you will need to travel to the mountain and the desert.

2. To do that first you need to get your magic. This is hidden in the forgotten forest. You will know when you enter it as it acts like a maze. There is only one right path to get to the eldest tree, source of the magic.
