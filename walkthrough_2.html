3. The correct path in the forgotten forest is: RIGHT > UP > UP With your newly acquired magic (press SPACE to activate), you are more powerful against enemies. Take advantage of this in your combat.

4. Now it's time to head to the mountain. Go left from the entrance of the forgotten forest and you will get to the lumberjack. He will talk about some old magic used on the spikes blocking the entrance... could that be your new magic?

5. At the top of the mountain kill the cyclope enemies to deactivate the ice artifact.

6. Head south from town to the desert and enter the area with the fire artifact. That mass of enemies shouldn't be any problem with your magic. The gate to the castle should now be open. Make sure to visit the shop in town before heading to the final battle.
